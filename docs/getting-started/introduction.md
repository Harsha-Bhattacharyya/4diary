# Introduction to 4Diary

Welcome to **4Diary** - a privacy-first, end-to-end encrypted note-taking application!

## What is 4Diary?

4Diary is an open-source note-taking application that prioritizes your privacy above all else. Every note you create is encrypted on your device before being sent to the server, ensuring that no one but you can read your content.

## Why Choose 4Diary?

Unlike traditional note-taking apps, 4Diary is built with privacy as the foundation:

- **Your Data, Your Control**: Self-host on your own server
- **Open Source**: Fully transparent FLOSS software
- **No Vendor Lock-in**: Export your data anytime
- **Privacy by Design**: Server cannot decrypt your notes
